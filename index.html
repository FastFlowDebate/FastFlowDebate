A tool for debaters made by debaters.

Cards

FastFlow replaces folder style organization with tag based searching, simply attach tags to cards and search for cards how you want to.

Speech

Whereas Word only offers you a texteditor for writing speeches, FastFlow gives you a tool for presenting speeches. Helping you ensure your speech is to time with links that make sure any file you reference is only one click away.

Flow

Fastflow's flowing replaces excel spreadsheets and word templates with an intuitive interface. Quickly add links to your cards and navigate through debate rounds.
